Restaurants

     The restaurant scene in Liverpool city region is booming, with more places to eat and more international cuisines than ever before. Our suggestion of restaurants includes The London Carriage Works, Fonseca's and Delifonseca Dockside.

  • The London Carriage Works

    The London Carriage Works restaurant is perfectly placed for visitors. TLCW serves modern British food with an emphasis on sourcing and using local, fresh and seasonal produce. The innovative menus are complemented by a carefully selected 200 bin wine list. TlCW is a destination restaurant in its own right but is also open for breakfast, brunch, lunch, afternoon tea, pre concert/theatre supper, dinner and post concert/theatre cocktails.

     

    Address: Hope Street Hotel, 40 Hope St, Liverpool L1 9DA, UK

    Phone: +44 151 705 2222

  • Fonseca's

    Ground floor deli with airy, stripped floor upstairs restaurant with booths, for Mediterranean food.

     

    Address: 12 Stanley St, Liverpool L1 6AF, UK

    Phone: +44 151 255 0808

  • Delifonseca Dockside

    Delifonseca Dockside is a ‘foodies’ paradise situated just outside of Liverpool City Centre. With a blackboard menu that changes daily, boasting the freshest produce from around the North West, the restaurant at Delifonseca Dockside combines classic cuisine with exciting new dishes in a welcoming and friendly atmosphere.

     

    Address: Brunswick Way, Liverpool L3 4BN, UK

    Phone: +44 151 255 0808
